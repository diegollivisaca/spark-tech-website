What We Do

Electrical Services for Your Home

From diagnosing mystery breaker trips to wiring a new EV charger in your garage — we handle every residential electrical need with care and expertise.

Electrical Troubleshooting

Tripping breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights, or GFCI faults? We quickly diagnose and resolve electrical issues to keep your home safe and powered.

Fast diagnosis & same-visit fix

Panel Upgrades

Replace your outdated 100A service with a modern 200A+ panel. Safer, code-compliant, and ready to handle today's electrical loads — from EV chargers to smart home systems.

200A upgrades · Permit included

EV Charger Installations

Charge your electric vehicle at home with a proper Level 2 EVSE install. We handle wiring, the permit, and setup for all makes and charger brands.

All EV brands supported

New Dedicated Circuits

Adding a home office, workshop, hot tub, or major appliance? We install protected dedicated circuits that deliver reliable power exactly where you need it.

20A / 30A / 50A circuits available

Outlets & Switches

GFCI outlets in bathrooms and kitchens, USB receptacles, smart switches, and more. Quick, clean installs with proper wiring — no wire nuts hanging loose.

GFCI · AFCI · Smart-home ready

Pre-sale & pre-purchase inspections
